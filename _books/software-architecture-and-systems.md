This collection deals with software architecture: the design of systems that have to keep working as the business scales, the team scales, and the requirements change underneath them.

Microservices are well represented here because shipping and operating distributed systems is now table stakes for most engineering leaders. The deeper material, on data, on domain modelling, on architecture that absorbs change without rewrites, is what separates teams that ship in year three from teams that rewrite in year three.

Blog posts and YouTube videos can be valuable sources of information, but I've found the signal-to-noise ratio there to be quite low. Peer-reviewed work that has gone through a publishing process tends to offer a much better return on investment.
