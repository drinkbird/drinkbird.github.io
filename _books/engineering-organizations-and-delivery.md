Engineering organisations are systems. Their performance, the speed at which they deliver value, the reliability of what they ship, the quality of the work life inside them, is the product of design choices that compound over years. These books are the canon of those choices: how to instrument, structure, and continuously improve a software-delivery organisation.

If you have authority over how software is built (CTO, VP, director, product leader, or staff engineer), these are the books that will help you exercise that authority well.

> We are turning the U.S. Air Force into a software company that happens to deliver airpower. <br>
> -- Adam Furtado, Chief Product Officer at [Kessel Run / U.S. Air Force](https://kesselrun.af.mil/), UXDX 2018

If the world's largest bureaucracy can undergo such a significant digital transformation, then so can your business.
