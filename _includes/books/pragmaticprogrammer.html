This was the first technical book I ever read, back in the early 2000's. I wasn't quite ready for it then, but some of its concepts stuck in my head and helped me become who I am today. Instead on focusing on code, the authors present a number of practical approaches they have found to work in real world scenarios, accompanied with solid advice and outstanding analogies. As [Ward Cunningham](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ward_Cunningham), the father of wiki, said: > If I'm putting together a project, it's the authors of this book that I want... And failing that I'd settle for people who've read their book."
