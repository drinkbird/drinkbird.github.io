In this book, legendary software expert Robert C. Martin (Uncle Bob) offers a comprehensive code of conduct for professional programmers, which includes disciplines, techniques, tools and practices of true software craftsmanship. Keep in mind that the wisdom contained within those pages has emerged through decades of real-world work experience, not in some vacuum. Of course, the reader doesn't have to agree with everything the book says. On the other hand, those who refuse to read such a book on "ideological grounds" have probably more reasons to worry about than the poor quality of their work.
